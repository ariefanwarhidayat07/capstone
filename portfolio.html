This interactive grouped bar chart visualizes Indonesia's trade with ASEAN countries from 2013 to 2022, highlighting trade values (exports and imports) for individual countries in the region.

Data Source: UN Comtrade

Interactive Features:

  • Year Slider: Adjust the year to explore trade values for a specific time period.
  • Country Selector: Focus on specific ASEAN countries by selecting them from the dropdown menu.
  • Data Highlights: Hover over bars to display detailed trade value information.
